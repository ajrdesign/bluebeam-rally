Teams

  • Anders Nystedt

    Driver / Co-driver

    The lifelong racing career of Anders Nystedt is packed full of excitement and speed. He started in a Saab V4 Standard B before switching to a brand new Opel Ascona A Rally and starting the Hemglass Racing Team for the Swedish Championship in Rallycross. Always focused on the next victory, Anders soon started another team called “Anders Kläder Racing”, which first competed in the Saab but soon switched to four rear wheel Toyota Starlets. Anders competed in the Suzuki Cup next with Stocksund Rally Team, highlighted by a second place finish in Group N of the 1989 Swedish Rally. Later, Anders and his friend Kjelle Friberg started JVC Rally Team, a fully-sponsored professional rally team that raced in the Swedish Championship and abroad. Anders’ most memorable moment with JVC occurred in the 1993 Swedish Rally, when Gigi Hamilton, singer of the hit Swedish pop group Freestyle, rode shotgun as the co-driver. Lucky Anders!

    High speed facts

    Strength
    An aggressive driver who always keeps his eye on the prize.
    Favorite interest
    Definitely boating on the Stockholm archipelago in the summer. I love swimming and playing water sports while looking at the beautiful shorelines of the coast. It’s absolutely amazing. In the winter, I’m all about downhill skiing. But that hobby is second, of course, to drifting in my rally car.
    Favorite Rally
    WRC: Networg-Q, England (Old RAC-Rally)
    Favorite Driver
    Sebastian Loeb

  • Björn Wulff

    Driver / Co-driver

    Racing is in Björn’s blood. He started at the age of 16, joining his father on a team that drove endurance races in a Saab 99, and later as a co-driver for both his father and his cousin. These races gave Björn a great opportunity to learn how to handle a car in high speed, and to find optional apices through corners. After sensing the rally heat and gaining a need for more speed, Björn struck out on his own path in the forest and competed in a standard Golf rally car before becoming the lead driver for Bluebeam Rally Sweden.

    High speed facts

    Strength
    Stays calm, cool and focused on the track.
    Favorite interest
    Heading to the gym and pressing play on my iPod. Working out to my playlist gives me a free zone to decompress out and relax – something that is really important when your schedule is as tight as mine.
    Favorite Rally
    In the WRC, I would have to say Rally Sweden and Jyväskylä Rally (In Finland). For the SM and DM I would say all the winter rallies north of Sandviken in February.
    Favorite Driver
    Colin McRae

  • Christian Gren

    Driver

    Christian’s interest in racing and rallying began at the age of 15, staying out late to watch late-night street races and local rally competitions. He started rallying a few years ago, beginning with classes to obtain the obligatory rally license and eventually his co-driver license (You hear that kids? It’s cool to follow the rules). Christian is excited to take rallying full speed with Bluebeam Rally Sweden, and participate for the first time as the driver in a rally competition.

    High speed facts

    Strength
    Quick to pick up new tricks, and never misses a beat.
    Favorite interest
    When I’m not rallying I like working on my every day car and my motorcycle, upgrading parts and making everything just a little bit better. Working out at the gym is also a big part of my life, and I try to go at least four times a week. I’ve also played a lot of golf. I started golfing when I was 5, and by the time I turned 20 I was on the Swedish National Tour.
    Favorite Rally
    The WRC in Finland 2009. Great event and very nice weather.

  • Jan Österman

    Driver / Co-driver

    Jan may be new to rallying but he’s definitely not new to fast cars; his roots are in the street racing circuit. For about 12 years his main passion was tuning and driving cars, but about five years ago Jan started spending more and more time on the tarmac. After many years of encouragement from his good friend and current Bluebeam Rally Sweden teammate, Björn, Jan finally converted to the woods and narrow gravel roads. Rallying first with Björn in a Volkswagen Golf – Class Rally Economy – and now with Bluebeam Rally Sweden. The race is on!

    High speed facts

    Strength
    Stays cool, calm and collected at every twist and turn.
    Favorite interest
    I love spending time in my house on the countryside. When I’m out there, you’ll either find me breaking waves in high speed on my Jet Ski, or fishing in a nice, quiet little bay with friends.
    Favorite Rally
    In WRC, my favorit Rally Sweden – hands down. Otherwise, I really like Pikes Peak. It may not be technically classified as a rally, but that doesn’t matter. It’s a blast!
    Favorite Driver
    Walter Röhlr
